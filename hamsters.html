About Hamsters

Russian Dwarf Hamsters:

Russian dwarf hamsters can live in pairs of the same gender. However, sometimes the hamsters won't get along and thus must be seperated. These hamsters are known to be big hoarders. They will often have their own "secret" stashes within their enclosures.

Russian dwarf hamsters are also known as winter white russian dwarf hamsters. They're called winter white because in the winter their coat turns white.

These kinds of hamsters are also nocturnal. They may come out in the daytime but theyre most active at night. They run on their hamster wheel at night and they can usually run for six miles.

Russian Dwarf Hamsters as Pets:

Dwarf hamsters are the hardest hamsters to tame. As it takes them longer to trust people. However, theyre naturally curious so they may come up to you on their own will.

They are also very playful, and they love chewing on stuff. Chewing on things may also include biting your finger. Sometimes its their way of playing. They are also speedy quick, so be careful not to drop them or they may make a run for it.
